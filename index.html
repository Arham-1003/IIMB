Welcome To Unmaad 2024

IIM Bangalore's annual cultural festival, "Unmaad 2024", is renowned as one of the country's biggest business school cultural festivals. The event is scheduled to take place offline for the first time in two years from February 23rd to February 25th, 2024, providing 3 days of uninterrupted entertainment.
Unmaad draws attendees from all across India and provides a platform to display your abilities in competitions that include, among other things, street dramas, musical and dance performances, quizzes, debates, and the grand Unmaad fashion show!
The event has hosted artists like Mohit Chauhan, The Yellow Diary, Shankar Ehsaan Loy, KK, Kailash Kher, Lucky Ali, Strings, Jethro Tull, the Indian Ocean, The Local Train, Agam, Benny Dayal, Salim-Sulaiman and Prateek Kuhad.
