Welcome To Unmaad 2024

IIM Bangalore's "Unmaad 2024" is one of India's largest business school cultural festivals, happening offline from Feb 23-25, 2024. It offers a variety of competitions such as street dramas, music, dance, quizzes, and debates, culminating in a grand fashion show.

The festival has featured famous artists like Mohit Chauhan, Shankar Ehsaan Loy, and Prateek Kuhad, attracting attendees nationwide.
